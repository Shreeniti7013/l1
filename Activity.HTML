Giant panda


The giant panda also known as the panda bear or simply panda,is a bear species endamic to China.
It is characterised by its white coat with black patches around the eyes,ears,legs and shoulders,Its body is rounded; adult individuals weight 100 to 115 kg (220 to 254 kg) and are typically 1,2 to 1,9 m (3ft 11inch to 6ft 3inch) they are very cute.They are friendly animal.
